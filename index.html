THE FUTURE OF BOSTON

Taking architectural cues from it's turn of the century neighbors in the Historic Aberdeen District of Brighton. The Aberdeen features "forever" skyline views along one of boston's most historic streets, Commonwealth Avenue, The Aberdeen will be Brighton's next great building.

Elegantly designed and luxuriously finished, The Aberdeen is a high end, boutique property with only 40 residences, coming soon for sale. Expertly designed inside and out with a striking modern twist on an Art Deco style, there is simply not a higher quality building in the area.

1650 Commonwealth Ave., Brighton, 857-383-3111.

LIFE AT YOUR PACE

Ideally situated on an open corner of the carriage lane off Commonwealth Avenue, the property is on the Brookline/Brighton border and just minutes to local area amenities such as Whole Foods, the Chestnut Hill Reservoir, Cleveland Circle, and Brookline's current "it" neighborhood of Washington Square.

ELEGANT FINISHES

The units offer central HVAC, in unit laundry, hardwood floors in the living rooms, designer finishes throughout the bathrooms and kitchens: inclusive of custom cabinetry, quartz counters, high end Jenn-Air appliances and Ann Sacks tile. The exclusive Penthouse units feature upgraded finishes and a Wolf and Sub Zero appliance package.
